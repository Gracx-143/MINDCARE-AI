### 3. 🌬️ Breathing Exercises (BRAND NEW!)
**Guided breathwork for instant stress relief**

Four scientifically-proven techniques:

#### 4-7-8 Breathing
- **Pattern:** Inhale 4s → Hold 7s → Exhale 8s
- **Benefits:** Reduces anxiety, improves sleep, lowers blood pressure
- **Best For:** Bedtime, anxiety attacks

#### Box Breathing (Navy SEAL)
- **Pattern:** Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Pause 4s
- **Benefits:** Enhances focus, reduces stress, improves performance
- **Best For:** High-pressure situations, before meetings

#### Calming Breath
- **Pattern:** Inhale 4s → Hold 2s → Exhale 6s → Pause 2s
- **Benefits:** Quick relaxation, stress relief, mental clarity
- **Best For:** Quick breaks, mild stress

#### Energizing Breath
- **Pattern:** Inhale 6s → Hold 2s → Exhale 4s
- **Benefits:** Increases energy, improves alertness
- **Best For:** Morning routine, fatigue

**Features:**
- 🎯 Visual animated breathing circle
- ⏱️ Automated timing
- 🔄 Cycle counter
- ⏸️ Pause/resume
- 🎨 Color-coded by technique
