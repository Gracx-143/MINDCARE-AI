## 🌬️ Breathing Exercises

Guided breathwork for instant stress relief and focus:

### 4-7-8 Breathing
- **Pattern:** Inhale 4s, Hold 7s, Exhale 8s
- **Benefits:** Reduces anxiety, improves sleep, lowers blood pressure
- **Best for:** Before bed, during anxiety

### Box Breathing (Navy SEAL Technique)
- **Pattern:** Inhale 4s, Hold 4s, Exhale 4s, Pause 4s
- **Benefits:** Enhances focus, reduces stress, improves performance
- **Best for:** High-pressure situations, before important tasks

### Calming Breath
- **Pattern:** Inhale 4s, Hold 2s, Exhale 6s, Pause 2s
- **Benefits:** Quick relaxation, stress relief, mental clarity
- **Best for:** Quick breaks, mild stress

### Energizing Breath
- **Pattern:** Inhale 6s, Hold 2s, Exhale 4s
- **Benefits:** Increases energy, improves alertness, mental boost
- **Best for:** Morning routine, fatigue, low energy

**Features:**
- Visual breathing guide (animated circle)
- Automated timing
- Cycle counter
- Pause/resume capability
- Multiple techniques
- Science-backed patterns
